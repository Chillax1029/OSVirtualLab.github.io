The Virtual Lab for Operating System Scheduling Algorithms offers an interactive platform to explore First-Come, First-Served (FCFS) and Shortest Job First (SJF) scheduling. Through real-time simulations, users gain insights into these algorithms' workings and real-world implications, enhancing their understanding of operating system scheduling.
